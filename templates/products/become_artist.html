Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Our team typically reviews applications within 7-10 business days. You'll receive an email notification once your application has been reviewed, regardless of the outcome.

VarnikaKart charges a 15% commission on each sale, which covers payment processing, customer service, and marketing. There are no monthly fees or listing fees.

You're responsible for shipping your products to customers. We provide shipping label generation and tracking integration. Our standard return policy is 7 days, but you can set specific policies for fragile or custom items.

Payments are processed every 15 days for all completed orders (after the return period). We offer direct bank transfers to your registered account. You can track all your earnings in real-time through your seller dashboard.

We provide dedicated seller support via email and phone, educational resources on product photography and descriptions, marketing opportunities, and a private community forum to connect with other artisans.
