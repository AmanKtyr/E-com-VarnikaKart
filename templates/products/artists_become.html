Frequently Asked Questions

There is no upfront fee to join VarnikaKart as an artist. We operate on a commission-based model, where we take a percentage of each sale. This way, we only succeed when you succeed.

Our team typically reviews applications within 7-10 business days. We carefully evaluate each submission to ensure the quality and authenticity of products on our platform.

As an artist, you're responsible for shipping your products to customers. You can set your shipping rates and policies in your store settings. VarnikaKart provides guidelines for shipping and returns to ensure a positive customer experience.

Payments are processed every two weeks for all completed orders. We offer multiple payment methods including direct bank transfers and digital payment platforms. You can track your earnings in real-time through your artist dashboard.

We provide comprehensive support including marketing assistance, professional product photography tips, pricing guidance, and dedicated customer service. We also offer educational resources to help you grow your business and improve your craft.
